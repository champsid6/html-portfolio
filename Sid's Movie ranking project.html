The Best Movies According To Sid

My top 3 movies of all time

Shawshank Redemption

This is my favorite movie because it has all the elements of a great movie, the acting is fabulous, the pace
is just superb and the ending is to die for

Pulp Fiction

This is one of my favorites because it was one of the first movies that broke norm, the structure of the movie
is just something I had never seen before yet it keeps me so captivated, the direction by Christopher Nolan is
brilliant, the perfomances by the cast is simply ten-on-ten and the background score is brilliant.

City of Angels

This movie simply is one of those movies for date night. Meg Ryan and Nicolas Cage are just electrifying together,
the ending is a bit teary eyed but I can see this movie another 100 time.
